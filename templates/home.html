About

Pollution and resource scarcity are becoming real problems here on Earth. Only abut 20-25% of people actually recycle effictively worldwide. EcoDollar is a non-profit organization aimed at helping people to recycle by giving them a motive to, and as a result making the world a better place, little by little.
